![Hackathon Banner](/images/blog/banner.webp)

On March 23-24, we hosted the inaugural annual hackathon event on Duke Kunshan University's campus, attended by over 100 participants, including 10 speakers from academia and industry, 8 mentors, and 4 judges. Student participants competed in teams of 2-4 people to build an AI/Web3 project within just 24 hours.

![Speakers](/images/blog/faculty.webp)

Three teams won the first-place prizes in Finance, Health, and Environment categories with exciting projects. You can view the projects gallery on our [DevPost website](https://lnkd.in/gC52MvZR).

![Student](/images/blog/student.webp)

### Company and University Representation

Some companies represented during the hackathon included Conflux Web3, Microsoft, Max Planck Institute, KPMG, L'Oréal, and Unilever. Universities represented included Duke Kunshan University, Xi'an Jiaotong-Liverpool University, and Tongji University.

We are immensely grateful to our sponsoring organizations, including Conflux company, DKU Undergraduate Studies, Division of Natural and Applied Sciences, InE, Student Affairs, and the organizer teams of DKU Computer Science Club and DKU Finance Club for making this event a reality.

We hope to see you at HackDKU 2025!
